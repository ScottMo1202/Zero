An Eco-friendly Lifestyle

Zero is an iOS mobile app that helps users track and manage perishable products such as food, cosmetics, and medications.

Get Zero for Free

Why Zero?

Have you ever bought products, forgotten about them, and found out later that they had expired?

According to our recent research, 85.7% of people have experienced the such an issue for food. In addition, 76.2% of people waste at least 10 dollars, monthly, due to expired products. We understand that you are busy and have all kinds of important things to worry about.

So let us help you manage expiration dates of your products and foster an eco-friendly lifestyle.

What Is Zero?

Zero is an IOS mobile application that encourages users to reduce waste by helping them manage and track expiration dates of perishable products such as food and cosmetics.

All you need are three little steps:

Sign up

Create an account today for FREE! No subscription fee needed

Scan

Scan barcodes, receipts, or manually input a expiration date by yourself

Get notifications

Ta-dah! Now just wait for your automatic reminder when the expiration date approaches
